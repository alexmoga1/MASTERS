What Is This?

🏆
The Goal

The Masters is a 4-round golf tournament at Augusta National. Your job is to build the best team of golfers possible. At the end of 4 rounds, whoever has the lowest combined team score wins the pool — and the prize money.

1
Pick one golfer from each tier

Tiers 1–4 are required. Tier 5 is optional but free upside — always pick one. Tier 1 = heavy favorites. Tier 5 = longshots with big potential.

2
How your team score is calculated

Each round, your team score = the sum of all your golfers' scores, minus the single worst score. So every round your worst golfer gets dropped — a safety net built in.

3
Golf scoring: lower is better

Scores are shown relative to par (e.g. -5 means 5 under par). The golfer who plays the fewest strokes wins. You want your team's total to be as far below par as possible.

4
Missed cuts get penalized

If your golfer misses the cut (eliminated after round 2), they receive a penalty score for rounds 3 & 4 — the average of the 10 worst active players that round. Pick wisely.

5
Strategy tip

Don't just pick the favorites. A Tier 1 lock is smart, but your Tier 4 & 5 picks are where you can separate yourself. A hot week from a longshot beats a boring safe pick every time.
