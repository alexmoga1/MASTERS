How It Works

1
Pick one golfer from each tier

Tiers 1–4 are required. Tier 5 is optional but can only help you.

2
Tiers are based on odds

Tier 1 = favorites, Tier 5 = longshots. Every team gets one elite pick and some underdogs.

3
Team score = sum of your golfers, minus the worst each round

Each round, the highest (worst) score among your golfers is dropped. Lower total wins.

4
Missed cuts get a penalty

If your golfer misses the cut, they receive the average of the 10 worst scores for rounds 3 & 4.

5
Pay your $7 buy-in
